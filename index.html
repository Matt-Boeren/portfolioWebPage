About me

Hi, I’m Matt Boeren, a 21-year-old software developer with a passion for solving problems through clean and efficient code. My journey began at 14 when I bought an Arduino kit for school, and ever since, I’ve been hooked on building projects that bring ideas to life.

Today, I specialize in back-end development, where I focus on making data transfer seamless, reliable, and optimized. I have hands-on experience with JavaScript, Java and PHP plus working knowledge of Swift and C++. On the hardware side, I’ve also built small projects using Arduino and Raspberry Pi. On the database side my knoledge goes out from classic SQL databases to NOSQL soulutions like mongoDB.

As a self-employed student developer, I’m eager to take on new challenges whether it’s building efficient back-end systems, creating custom solutions, or contributing to innovative projects. If you’re looking for someone motivated, versatile, and ready to deliver, I’d love to collaborate.
