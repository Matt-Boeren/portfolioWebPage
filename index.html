About me

Hi there! I’m Matt Boeren, a 20-year-old software developer. My journey into programming began at the age of 14 when I purchased an Arduino kit for school. Quickly, I started creating my own projects with it, and from that moment on, I was hooked on coding. Today, my passion lies in back-end development, where I enjoy the challenge of efficiently transferring data from one point to another in the right format. I have experience in JavaScript, Java, PHP, and Python, along with some knowledge of Swift and C++. Additionally, I’ve made some small hardware projects using Arduino and Raspberry Pi. If you’d like to connect or learn more about my work, feel free to reach out!
